Public Adjuster - FLORIDA, TEXAS, AND PENNSYLVANIA

Job description

If You are dedicated and ambitious, iFile Claims Adjusters is an excellent place to grow your career. Do you have your 3-20 or 6-20 license? Then we are looking for You! Self-driven “go-getters'' who can thrive in a young and fast paced work environment. Our dedicated office staff is always there to support our adjusters with the claim from beginning to end.

Responsibilities

As a Public Adjuster your responsibilities include but are not limited to:

    Conduct a FREE inspection of the property to determine circumstances and coverage options of the possible claim.

  • ► Explain to the Client the claim process and keep close contact with them throughout the whole claim.
  • ► Collect all required claim documents to submit the claim.
  • ► Attend all inspections necessary.
  • ► Keep an open communication with the Insurance carrier during the claim.
  • ► Negotiate a fair settlement with the Insurance Adjuster on behalf of the Client.
  • ► Maintain a continuous connection with the Client, informing them about the development of their claim, including the status of negotiations and settlement offers.

Fluency in Creole or Spanish is a Plus

Experience with estimating software (Xactimate) and scoping is preferred but not required
